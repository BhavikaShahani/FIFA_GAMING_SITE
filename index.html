How the Game Works?

A Simple Guide


Bid on Card Packs

You have to predict the prices of card packs based on their description and bid on card packs


Build A Team

Once you collect 11 or more cards, your team will begin to form and you will join the leaderboard of players from across the world trying to get the best players


Trade Cards

Once you have built a team you can trade cards with other players to build your dream team


Specialised Cards

Each player card is specialised and unique. Player attributes specifications allow you to get in depth comparison of players


Competitive Bidding

Bids on card packs will be intense as the bid with closest value to the actual value of the card pack, wins the card pack


Play and compete

Compare your team with others and outbid them to earn more and bid more
